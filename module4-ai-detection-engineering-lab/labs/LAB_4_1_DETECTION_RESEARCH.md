# Lab 4.1: LLM Assisted Detection Research

## Goal

Turn analyst selected detection sources into a structured research brief that separates evidence, limitations, assumptions, and test hypotheses.

## Workbench steps

1. Open `Detection Research` at http://localhost:8501.
2. Select the encoded PowerShell scenario.
3. Review at least three detections on detection.fyi.
4. Check the original repository where it is available.
5. Record what each source detects, its required fields, filters, limits, false positives, and test evidence.
6. Add your findings to the editable source notes.
7. Generate the research brief with the LLM.
8. Correct unsupported claims and save the reviewed artifact.

## Exit criteria

- At least three sources were compared.
- Stable behavior was separated from brittle indicators.
- Unknowns and assumptions are explicit.
- Positive and negative test hypotheses exist.
- `workspace/encoded_powershell_research.json` is saved.
