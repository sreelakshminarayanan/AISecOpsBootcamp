# Lab 4.2: Schema Grounded Detection Design

## Goal

Use the reviewed research brief, scenario evidence, and allowed ECS schema to create a testable detection design.

## Workbench steps

1. Open `Detection Design`.
2. Select the encoded PowerShell scenario.
3. Review the scenario and available ECS fields.
4. Confirm the reviewed research artifact is present.
5. Generate the structured design with Ollama.
6. Review required fields, logic, exclusions, ATT&CK mappings, assumptions, gaps, and tests.
7. Remove unsupported fields or claims.
8. Save the design and run schema validation.

## Exit criteria

- Every required field exists in the supplied schema.
- Every ATT&CK mapping has evidence and confidence.
- Known gaps and assumptions are explicit.
- Positive and negative tests are defined.
- `workspace/encoded_powershell_design.json` passes validation.
